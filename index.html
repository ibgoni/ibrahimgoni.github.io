About

I am a PhD candidate in Development Economics at the Centre d'Études et de Recherches sur le Développement International (CERDI), Université Clermont Auvergne (UCA), affiliated with CNRS and IRD.

My research focuses on the economics of digital transitions, agricultural transformation, and ecological trade-offs in Sub-Saharan Africa. I study how digital technologies reshape rural economies, how agricultural systems adapt to environmental pressures, and the interactions between economic development and ecological sustainability.

Research Interests

  • Development Economics
  • Digital Transitions & ICT for Development
  • Agricultural Economics & Rural Transformation
  • Environmental & Ecological Economics
  • Sub-Saharan Africa
